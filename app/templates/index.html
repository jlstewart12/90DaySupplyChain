90 Day Supply Chain

The Commodity Truck Volume reported by the USDA. United States Dept of Agriculture (USDA).
The visualization displays data during the last 60 days of commodity supplies being delivered throughout the United States.
The results are inconclusive as to there being a shortage or delay of resources being transported and made available for consumption. Only conclusion I can discern is that commodity transport volume of supplies have remained consistant during the pandemic of 2020 to current status.
